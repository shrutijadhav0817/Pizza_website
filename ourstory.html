Our Story - La Pino'z Pizza

OUR STORY

The story of India’s fastest-growing pizza place started back in 2011 when Sanam Kapoor opened his very first pizzeria in Chandigarh. Inspired by his own concept and love for pizza, he wanted to introduce a better way of doing pizza – ambitious, fast, and unique. He envisioned another pizzeria but maybe this time, something that would redefine exactly what pizza meant to everyone.

And so, La Pino’z Pizza was born. The response was great, and soon Sanam was opening back-to-back outlets. His momentum grew stronger, and there was no looking back! As of today, La Pino’z has 700+ outlets across India and with the recent one opening in London, the brand is going global.
